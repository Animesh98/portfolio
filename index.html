03 — About

Who I am, what I do.

I like building the unglamorous middle of AI systems — the retrieval that doesn't hallucinate, the eval that tells the truth, the integration that survives a real customer. Models are the easy part.

My stack is breadth over depth on purpose: a production LLM bug can live in the prompt, the retriever, the API surface, the queue, the DB — and I'd rather be the person who can walk the whole trace than one who owns just one layer.

Nights and weekends I run fine-tunes on a 16 GB card in my bedroom and write down the regressions. I'd rather ship a boring mechanism that works than a clever one that needs an explanation.
